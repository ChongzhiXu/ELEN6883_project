About our work:
We are Columbia Engineering students, and this is our ELEN E6883 (Introduction to Blockchain Technology) course project. We developed a DAPP to establish a crypto art market for sticker transactions. In our DAPP, sticker creators can sell their works while far-sighted collectors can mine and buy original works certified on the blockchain. This DAPP is deployed on test-network2.0 Huygens of ComputeCoin Network (CCN) which is the next generation infrastructure that supports common Web3.0 and Metaverse services.
